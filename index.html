About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Jackson Gilliam, and I am a student, photographer, and aspiring politician living in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I am currently a high school student at Thomas Edison Preparatory School. Outside of the classroom, I am an avid traveller, as well as a tennis player, and have been involved in academic bowl for six years now. I am also a "spotter", which is someone who takes pictures of airplanes, and is well informed when it comes to aviation in general. I am also very interested in politics, and will have a civil discussion with anyone over any topic. In fact, my goal in life, as far as careers go, is to attend an esteemed school, and obtain a degree in political science, and to eventually be a congressman, and represent Oklahoma's 1st Congressional District in the U.S. House Of Representatives. For now, I am focusing on my education, in hopes of recieving an academic scholarship to attend my dream school, the University Of California, Los Angeles.
